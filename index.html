The Philosophy

Where Precision Roasting Meets Architectural Minimalism

We source exclusively from single-estate biodynamic farms above 1,800 meters. Every micro-lot is roasted inside custom modified cast-iron drums, controlling airflow down to the millisecond to preserve the delicate floral esters and native origin characteristics.

Our spaces reflect our brew guidelines: clean, uncompromised, balanced, and intended to cultivate intentional moments of tactile connection.
